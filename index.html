Our believes

The hurtling pace of scientific development can make it feel as if humans have been exploring space longer than the 61 years since the launch of the first satellite, Sputnik 1.Space exploration isn’t simply a sign of humanity’s hubris or a brazen desire to find new places to live and new sources of wealth. If we take the risk of venturing beyond our terrestrial home, it’s also to learn more about ourselves and our planet, improve life on Earth, and maybe, just maybe, find or create a new future for our children
